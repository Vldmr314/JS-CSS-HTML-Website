Random Cocktail Recipe

Balmoral

Ingredients:

Scotch, Sweet Vermouth, Dry Vermouth, Bitters

Instruction:

In a mixing glass half-filled with ice cubes, combine all of the ingredients. Stir well. Strain into a cocktail glass.
